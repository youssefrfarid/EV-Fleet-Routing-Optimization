\section{Introduction}\label{sec1}
This report investigates recent optimization approaches for coordinating electric vehicle (EV) fleets with realistic charging and routing constraints. The review summarizes five key studies from 2018 to 2025 that address aspects of the Electric Vehicle Routing Problem (EVRP) using metaheuristic or hybrid optimization techniques. Insights from these works are used to extract the essential formulation components---objective functions, decision variables, and constraints---that will guide the present project's mathematical model.
